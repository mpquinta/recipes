Loco Moco

Description

A traditional Hawaiian dish; there are many variations of it, though they mainly are composed of white rice topped with a hamburger steak, egg, and gravy sauce. Though other variations include bacon, chili, spam, ham, pork, sausage, teriyaki beef/chicken, shrimp, oysters, and other meat or seafood Loco Moco.
